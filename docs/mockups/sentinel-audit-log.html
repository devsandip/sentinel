  • Click any row to open its detail block. Click a control chip to see what it is.
  • The posture segmented control and the four selectors filter the table live.
  • Theme toggle top right; the screen is specified for light and dark.
  • What the screen deliberately does not have: date range, free-text search, pagination, a hash-chain integrity claim, or a tokens/cost column. Reasons are in the feature doc.
